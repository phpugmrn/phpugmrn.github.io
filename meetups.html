Sylius Days 2024

18. April 2024
Stephan Hochdörfer

Jointly together with our sponsor bitExpert AG we'll organize the second edition of Sylius Days on April 18-19 in Mannheim.

The first day will include interactive workshops to help you better understand the Sylius platform. This will be followed by a day filled with insightful presentations, networking opportunities, and of course, an after-party!

Interested in contributing or sponsoring? Don't hesitate to reach out to the Sylius or the bitExpert team.
